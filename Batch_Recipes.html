⚡ Parallel Prep Guide — All 28 Batches

When doing ALL batches at once, shared ingredients are prepped once. Frozen meat must be thawed the day before!

DAY BEFORE Thaw frozen items

  • ❄️ Pulled Chicken (3kg), Pork Carnitas (3kg), Chargrilled Chicken (8kg) → fridge
  • ❄️ Avocado Cubes (200g), Mango (150g) → fridge

0–10 min Shared fresh prep

  • 🌿 Wash + chop parsley — 1kg covers: Avocado Dressing, Chimichurri, Magic Green, Mushroom Mix, Bean Mix, Sweet & Spicy Mango
  • 🧅 Dice red onion — for: Avocado Dressing, Chimichurri, Piri Piri
  • 🍅 Dice tomatoes — for: Avocado Dressing, Chimichurri
  • 🥒 Grate + squeeze cucumber — for: Tzatziki
  • 🌶️ Chop Chili Rawit — for: Piri Piri Homemade

10–15 min Start bulgur + open cans

  • 🫘 Pour hot water over bulgur, let it soak (does itself!)
  • 🥫 Open + drain ALL cans: Kidney Beans, Chickpeas, Sweet Corn, Mushrooms, Beetroot, Jalapeños, Pepper Strips, Roasted Red Peppers

15–40 min Mix & blend everything

  • 🥣 Blend all sauces/dressings: Avo Dressing, Buffalo, Spudz Sauce, Chimichurri, Chipotle, Magic Green, Lemon Vinaigrette, Sriracha Lime, Sweet & Spicy Mango, Piri Piri
  • 🥣 Greek dips: Tzatziki, Tahini, Tirokafteri, Htipiti, Skordalia, Pantzarosalata, Flying Greek
  • 🥣 Greek dressings: Oregano Vin., Greek Vin., Lemon-Herb, Herbed Yogurt
  • 🫘 Bean Mix + Mushroom Mix: dump cans + seasoning/sauce, stir

40–55 min Meat batches + portioning

  • 🍗 4 meat batches: thawed meat + sauce, mix (~2 min each)
  • 📦 Portion into GN containers, label, date
  • 🧹 Clean up
